Ingredients

  • 100g Dark chocolate
  • 50g Unsalted butter
  • 2 Eggs
  • 20g Sugar
  • 30g Cake flour

How to cook

  1. Cut 100g dark chocolate into small pieces
  2. boil water and put heat‐resistant container with cut chocolate in it.
  3. put 50g unsalted butter into the container and mix them until they gets smooth
  4. Cold off the container
  5. put 2 eggs and 20g sugar into the new container and mix them
  6. Add melted chocolate into it and mix them
  7. Shift 30g cake flour over it and mix them
  8. put them into the container and bake at 180℃ for 10 minutes
